About Me

Hello and Welcome!

My name is Winnie Shi and I’m a finance enthusiast. This blog is my way of combining my excitement for teaching others with my interest in the industry. I don’t feel qualified to give financial advice, but 15 years of education (and counting) has taught me how to read, digest, and write fairly well – so through this blog, I put my decade-and-a-half-long education to good use.


Let’s unlock the seemingly fancy, complex finance industry together. Feel free to send me a message!
